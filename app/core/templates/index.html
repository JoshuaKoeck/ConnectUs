The Problem

Students in East and South Asia face significant barriers to developing real-life English fluency. High costs of language courses, limited access to native speakers, and lack of practical speaking opportunities prevent millions of students from achieving their educational and career goals.

‚ú®

Our Solution

ConnectUS creates a free virtual platform that connects English-speaking students from developed countries with Asian students. Through structured conversation sessions and cultural exchanges, we're making quality language practice accessible to everyone while fostering global understanding.
